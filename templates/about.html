About Visa Status Checker

The Visa Status Checker is a user-friendly web application that allows visa applicants to easily check their visa application status and receive important information about their application.

Our service provides real-time updates on visa application statuses, calculates working days since the application date, and sends automated email notifications to keep applicants informed throughout the process.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find my IRL number?
Your IRL (Indian Resident Label) number can be found on your visa application receipt or confirmation email.
How long does it take to process a visa application?
Processing times vary depending on the type of visa and current workload. Typically, it takes 5-15 working days.
What should I do if my application is rejected?
If your application is rejected, you will receive an email with further instructions. You may need to contact our office for more information and to discuss your options.
